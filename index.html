Preface

What are you doing here?

Once upon a time, I've finally landed a job in the field that I studied for and was truly interested in. 9 months pass and after the unfortunate cancellation of the project I was hired for, I was laid off. What I used to do for a living has now been successfully demoted into a hobby. It may forever remain a hobby, who knows. For the time being, while my unemployed self tries to look for employment that is even remotely related (or not), I shall candidly document here my attempts at making this time productive, whether it be successful or not.

What's in here?

You know how when you learn something, all is well and good except after a couple of years you realize you only have a vague recollection left. Yes, that. What I put on here, I probably won't encounter in my day to day. I don't want to completely forget what I've learned though, so here is my attempt to immortalize it in some way.

No but really, what's in here?

Logs, logs, logs. Whatever I think up. Logs that portray my thought process and how I pick at and (maybe) solve a problem.

I am someone who randomly came across your site

Hello Stranger, I wasn't expecting you. Pardon my gibberish and feel free to peruse my content. I hope you find it helpful. I have chosen to not share my contact details at the moment so if you have questions or clarifications regarding what I put on here, I'm sorry. I cannot help you. Google is your friend. I wish you luck.
